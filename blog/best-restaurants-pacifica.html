15 Best Restaurants in Pacifica You Need to Try

March 3, 2026 · By Coastal Catch Agency · 8 min read

Home / Pacifica Guide / Best Restaurants

Pacifica might be a small coastal town, but don't let that fool you. Tucked between the cliffs and the Pacific Ocean, our little stretch of Highway 1 is home to some seriously good food. Whether you're craving fresh-off-the-boat seafood, authentic Mexican, or a perfect cup of chowder with an ocean view, Pacifica delivers.

As locals who work with Pacifica businesses every day, we've eaten our way through town so you don't have to guess. Here are the 15 restaurants that keep us coming back.

Oceanfront Dining

Seafood · American

1. Nick's Rockaway

The crown jewel of Pacifica dining. Perched right on Rockaway Beach, Nick's serves up fresh seafood with panoramic ocean views that make every meal feel like a vacation. The cioppino is legendary, and their weekend brunch draws crowds from all over the Bay Area. Get there early or expect a wait -- it's worth it.

📍 100 Rockaway Beach Ave 💰 $$-$$$ ⭐ Best for: Date night, special occasions
Seafood · Casual

2. Moonraker Restaurant

Another oceanfront gem with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the Pacific. Moonraker has been a Pacifica institution for decades. The clam chowder is thick, creamy, and made fresh daily. Their sunset happy hour is one of the best-kept secrets on the coast.

📍 105 Rockaway Beach Ave 💰 $$-$$$ ⭐ Best for: Happy hour, ocean views

Casual Favorites

Mexican

3. Taqueria Pacifica

Ask any local where to get the best burrito in town and they'll point you here. No frills, no fancy decor, just incredible carne asada, handmade tortillas, and salsa that packs a punch. The super burrito is enormous and under $12. This is Pacifica comfort food at its finest.

📍 Palmetto Ave 💰 $ ⭐ Best for: Quick lunch, takeout
Pizza · Italian

4. Colombo's Delicatessen

A Pacifica institution since the old days. Colombo's makes hefty Italian sandwiches, fresh pasta salad, and some of the best pizza in town. The line at lunch tells you everything you need to know. Grab a sandwich and eat it on the beach.

📍 Palmetto Ave 💰 $ ⭐ Best for: Sandwiches, beach picnic
Seafood · Fish & Chips

5. Pacifica Pier Fish & Chips

You can't visit Pacifica without eating fish and chips near the pier. Crispy battered cod, hand-cut fries, and the sound of waves crashing. Simple, satisfying, and exactly what coastal living should taste like.

📍 Near Pacifica Municipal Pier 💰 $ ⭐ Best for: Families, tourists, quick bites
Breakfast · American

6. Breakers Cafe

The go-to breakfast spot. Pancakes that hang off the plate, strong coffee, and friendly service. Breakers has that classic diner energy where everyone knows everyone. Weekend mornings get packed, but the wait moves fast.

📍 Pedro Point Shopping Center 💰 $ ⭐ Best for: Breakfast, family meals

International Flavors

Thai

7. Thai Nakorn

Authentic Thai food that rivals anything in SF, right here in Pacifica. The pad thai is perfectly balanced, the green curry has real heat, and the tom kha soup will warm your soul on foggy Pacifica evenings. Great for takeout too.

📍 Palmetto Ave 💰 $$ ⭐ Best for: Takeout, date night
Chinese · Cantonese

8. Happy Garden

A local favorite for Chinese food that hits different. Generous portions, reasonable prices, and dishes that go beyond the typical takeout menu. The salt and pepper shrimp is a must-order.

📍 Manor Dr 💰 $$ ⭐ Best for: Family dinner, takeout
Indian

9. Curry by the Sea

Rich, flavorful curries with a view (well, almost). This newer addition to the Pacifica food scene has quickly built a loyal following. The butter chicken is creamy perfection, and their naan comes fresh from the tandoor.

📍 Linda Mar 💰 $$ ⭐ Best for: Dinner, spice lovers

Coffee & Bakeries

Coffee · Cafe

10. Peet's Coffee (Linda Mar)

Okay, it's a chain, but this particular Peet's has become a Pacifica community hub. Surfers in wetsuits grabbing post-session lattes, remote workers claiming window seats, neighbors catching up. The location makes it special.

📍 Linda Mar Shopping Center 💰 $ ⭐ Best for: Coffee meetings, morning ritual
Bakery · Cafe

11. Pacifica Bakery

Fresh-baked bread, pastries, and cakes that taste like they came from someone's grandma's kitchen. The cinnamon rolls are legendary on Saturday mornings. Support local bakers.

📍 Manor Dr 💰 $ ⭐ Best for: Weekend treats, birthday cakes

Hidden Gems

Mediterranean

12. Camellia Mediterranean

Fresh hummus, perfectly grilled kebabs, and warm pita that makes you close your eyes and drift to the coast of Turkey. This spot flies under the radar but delivers every single time.

📍 Palmetto Ave 💰 $$ ⭐ Best for: Healthy eating, lunch
American · Pub

13. Winters Tavern

Pacifica's favorite dive bar doubles as a surprisingly good food spot. Burgers, wings, and cold beer with live music on weekends. It's where locals go to be locals. Don't expect fancy; expect fun.

📍 Palmetto Ave 💰 $ ⭐ Best for: Live music, casual hangout
Filipino

14. Pacific Island Grill

Filipino comfort food that fills your belly and your heart. Adobo, lumpia, and pancit made with love. A community gem that brings island flavors to our little coastal town.

📍 Manor District 💰 $ ⭐ Best for: Family meals, comfort food
Mexican · Seafood

15. Puerto 27

Peruvian-Mexican fusion with creative cocktails and a vibe that says "we take food seriously but not ourselves." The ceviche is bright and fresh, the fish tacos are next level, and the margaritas are dangerous in the best way.

📍 Eureka Square 💰 $$ ⭐ Best for: Cocktails, creative dining

The Bottom Line

Pacifica's food scene punches way above its weight. You don't need to drive to San Francisco for a great meal -- just look around your own town. These 15 spots represent the best of what our community has to offer.
