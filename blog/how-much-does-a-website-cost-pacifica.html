Breaking Down Each Option

Option 1: DIY Website Builders (Wix, Squarespace, GoDaddy)

If you're tech-savvy and have time, DIY platforms are cheap upfront. But "cheap" has a catch:

  • Wix costs $16–$45/month — that's $192–$540/year, every year, forever
  • You spend 10–40 hours building it (what's your time worth?)
  • Templates look generic — customers can tell
  • SEO settings are limited; you'll still rank below a professionally optimized site
  • No one to call when something breaks

Good for: Hobbyists, very early-stage businesses, or owners who genuinely enjoy this stuff.

Option 3: Freelancers

Hiring a freelancer from Upwork or Fiverr can work — but it's a gamble. Quality varies wildly, communication is hit-or-miss, and you have no recourse if they disappear. Typical cost: $300–$1,500 for a basic site, 1–4 weeks to deliver.

Option 4: Traditional Web Agencies

Big agencies in San Francisco or San Mateo typically charge $2,000–$10,000+ for a small business website. They have high overhead, account managers, and design teams. For a restaurant or salon in Pacifica, you're paying for their downtown office rent. For enterprise clients, it makes sense. For local small businesses, it's overkill.

Hidden Costs to Watch For

🔍 What to Ask Any Web Designer Before You Hire Them

  • Is hosting included, or do I pay separately?
  • Who owns the domain name?
  • How much do updates cost after launch?
  • What happens if I want to cancel?
  • Is the site mobile-friendly and Google-optimized?

Common hidden costs from other providers:

  • Domain name: $12–$20/year (we can transfer yours or register a new one)
  • Hosting: $100–$300/year if not included
  • SSL certificate: Free with most modern hosting, but some charge $50–$100/year
  • Ongoing edits: Some agencies charge $75–$150/hour for changes after launch
  • Stock photos: Some designers charge extra; we use free sources or your own photos

At Coastal Catch, the price you see is the price you pay. If you add monthly maintenance ($97/mo), that includes hosting, unlimited text and image edits, and priority support. No hidden fees.
